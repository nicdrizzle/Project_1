THE STORY




Although being born in Argentina, I grew up here in Sydney. Cooking Asado is an Argentinian custom that is passed from the elders to the youth. I have always been passionate about the history of my culture and with that grew my enthusiasm for such a special craftsmanship.

Asados are cooked to celebrate life, death, new jobs, new opportunities, and even love. It brings people together, whether they are close to you or even people you may have just met. Ultimately my purpose and what I want to achieve is social connections with good food and long lasting memories.

It's not just cooking meat on a bbq, it's a culture, passion, history and a story.

Join my experience!
